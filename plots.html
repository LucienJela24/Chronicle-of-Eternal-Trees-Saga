Plots & Story Arcs

The turning points that shaped the Eternal Tree Saga

Cosmic Events

The Void Wars

Before Time

The Earlier Gods united to push back the Void (anti-relation) from consuming creation. All three factions—Dominator, Intervenor, and Observer—contributed to forging the Firmament.

Significance: Created the boundary between existence and non-existence

The Civil War

Before Time

After the Void Wars, Dominators and Intervenors turned on the Observers, destroying the Wild of Aletheia. Aletheia transformed destruction into new cosmology.

Significance: Led to the creation of the Two Trees and the Five Personas

The Seeding

Abs. 1

The Last Observer planted two seeds from the fruit of Intervenor essence, tempered by Observer detachment. The Tree of Order (Eastern) and Tree of Chaos (Western) grew.

Significance: Established the Two Planes and the Blood-River between them

Eastern Plane Conflicts

First Hollow Breach

Abs. 3,712

First manifestation of Void-touched scouts (Hollow) in the Eastern Plane. Gallian led the first coordinated defense.

Significance: Revealed the Grenlian threat; led to renewal of Covenant

War of the Grenlians

Abs. 7,585-9,588

Six thousand years of warfare against Ordered Void manifestations. The 5-Star Battalion was founded and sealed.

Significance: Three Guardians corrupted into Dark Emperors

Conclave of Shadows

Abs. 9,588-9,590

Formal alliance between Four Covert Orders and Four Allied Races. Celestine exiled to Dark Realm.

Significance: Established unified resistance against Grey Tide

Rite of Balancing

Abs. 23,507

Performed at Sanctuary of Eight Paths to strengthen seals. Catastrophically destabilized instead. Lucius Arogzan born in aftermath.

Significance: Birth of the Wanderer; Aneian homeworld consumed

Dark Emperors' Rise

Abs. 23,508-23,750

Tomoyama, Kross, and Marcus broke free from Tartarus, building the Dread Horde. Two-century war across Four Realms.

Significance: Tested the Guardian Order; Lucius's emergence

Wanderer's Path

Abs. 23,751-23,755

Lucius walked the eight-clue spiral path through all realms, meeting the Fourth Lord at the Sanctuary.

Significance: Lucius received the Essence of WILL; became 25th Guardian

Inquisitor's Reign

Abs. 33,965-34,018

Grand Marshal Redford Heinsforth exceeded mandate, conducting retroactive arrests, executions, and experiments on Aneian prisoners.

Significance: Demonstrated dangers of unchecked investigative power

Accord of Arcadia

Abs. 34,234

New covenant ratified establishing Council and Tribune as separate but covenantally linked. Watchers' Charter formalized.

Significance: Established Guardian Domain as neutral 'Fifth Realm'

Western Plane Conflicts

Great Decay

Abs. 20,203

First Metaphysical Embargo imposed on Xi Dynasty for perverting cultivation into rigid hierarchy.

Significance: Demonstrated Library's power to withdraw knowledge

The Great Divide

Abs. 28,223

Dimensional rift severed all land transport between Eastern and Western Continents.

Significance: Isolated the two Planes for millennia

Library War

Abs. 39,668-39,675

Jurisdictional schism between Vice-Chair and Chairman over preferential access. Lucien imposed Concordance of Jurisdictions.

Significance: Established Triadic Governance permanently

Burning of Xeria

Abs. 47,430

First Unbecoming manifestation when Tellerium attempted to digitize The Lost into Paraformer Arrays.

Significance: Revealed the chaotic Plenitude threat

Two-Millennia War

Abs. 47,502-49,634

Tellerium High Society vs. United Realms. Tellerium weaponized controlled Unbecoming but lost control.

Significance: Led to Trials of Tellerium and Union proclamation

Trials of Tellerium

Abs. 49,636

War crimes trials led by Valeria Scipio. Helena Cross and Kael Varus executed; others imprisoned.

Significance: Established post-war justice framework

The Convergence

The Convergence Approaches

Abs. 51,235+

The Three Trees (Order, Chaos, Old Tree) prepare to meet. Lucius and Lucien reunite in the Meadow Realm. The boundary between Planes begins to dissolve.

Significance: The center holds. For now. For always.

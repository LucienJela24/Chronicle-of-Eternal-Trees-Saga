Plots & Story Arcs

The complete chronicle — from the Void Wars to the Convergence of Three Trees

Expanded with lore from The Silent Growth, The Library Wars, and the Revelation at the Meadow

Cosmic Genesis & The Primordial Era

The Void Wars & The Civil War

Before Time

The Earlier Gods — Dominators (Cosmarchos), Intervenors (Symphion), Observers (Aletheia) — unite to forge the Firmament against the anti‑relation of the Void. But after victory, Dominators and Intervenors turn on the Observers, destroying the Wild of Aletheia. Aletheia’s final working transforms destruction into the Five Personas and the Blood‑River.

Significance: Establishes the boundary between existence and non‑existence; creates the ontological seeds for Order and Chaos.

The Seeding (Absolute Year 1)

Abs. 1

The Last Observer plants two seeds from the fruit of Intervenor essence, tempered by Observer detachment. The Tree of Order takes root in the Eastern Plane; the Tree of Chaos in the Western Plane. The Blood‑River flows between them, and the Old Tree (remnant of the Wild) is preserved in the Meridian Domain, guarded by the Fourth Lord.

Significance: Foundation of the Two Planes and the hidden Sanctuary that will host the Convergence.

The Silent Growth (Abs. 1 – 2,585)

Primordial Era (Eastern)

Emergency institutionalization: the First People (Lux‑Children, Stone‑Blooded, Echo‑Born, Number‑Touched) form survival cults that harden into the Four Realms. Aris the Sorrowful establishes the Cult of the Eternal Flame; the Five Primal Shapers become the Great Houses; Glaston’s Fivefold Binding cages wild magic; Helron the Measurer builds the Null Chamber. The Covenant of Lies (Abs. 2,584) seals the violent origins, and the Aneians are exiled to a pocket dimension.

Significance: The “Noble Withdrawal” is a myth — the realms are born of necessity and suppression, setting the stage for the conflicts of Books 1–5.

Eastern Plane Chronicles (Books 1–6)

The First Covenant & The Hollow Breach

Abs. 2,586 – 3,712

Gallian gathers the first eight Guardians at Arcadia, forging the Covenant “to the space between.” Master Oren teaches the Eye Practice. In Abs. 3,712, the first Hollow (Void‑touched scout) tears through the Meridian Threshold. Gallian leads the defense, and the Four Covert Orders are founded: Order of the Star (North), Root Sanctuary (East), Havenfire Guild (West), Filwind Institute (South).

Significance: The center holds, but the Grenlian threat is only beginning.

War of the Grenlians & The 5‑Star Battalion

Abs. 7,585 – 9,588

Six thousand years of warfare against Ordered Void manifestations. The 5‑Star Battalion (100,000 Void‑adapted warriors) is formed and later sealed near the Firmament. Three Guardians — Tomoyama, Kross, Marcus — are corrupted by purity‑without‑relation and become the first Dark Emperors. They are sealed in Tartarus by the Legendary Guardians Seraph, Kael, and Mordecai.

Significance: Institutional brittleness leads to the fall of heroes; the pattern of “purity creates vacuums” is proven.

Conclave of Shadows & The Grey Tide

Abs. 9,588 – 9,590

The Grey Tide — mist that falls upward, silence that swallows meaning — attacks all realms. The Four Covert Orders formally ally with the Ethereals, Drakes, Mystics, and Dromes. Celestine accepts exile to the Dark Realm as a secret mission. The Octagram secret society is founded to preserve the Eye Practice.

Significance: Unity through shadow; the orders learn that light without depth is blindness.

The Dark Emperors & The Wanderer’s Birth

Abs. 23,507 – 23,750

The Rite of Balancing catastrophically destabilizes the seals. Lucius Arogzan is born in the aftermath, marked by Void‑touch and haunted by inexplicable dreams of shelves, lenses, and a robed keeper. The three Dark Emperors break free and wage a two‑century war. Lucius, trained at the School of Arcadia, rises through the Guardian Order and helps defeat them — not by destruction, but by relation.

Significance: Birth of the Wanderer, who carries the memory of a lost teacher across death and rebirth.

The Wanderer’s Path (Eight Clues)

Abs. 23,751 – 23,755

Lucius walks the eight‑clue spiral: Shadow (Pact), Breath (Self), Wind (World), Depth (Inheritance), Uncertainty (Science), Absence (Intuition), Transcendence, and Center (Artifice). At the Sanctuary, the Fourth Lord reveals the Five Personas and grants him the Essence of WILL. He becomes the 25th Guardian and begins training Gael and Ren in the Eye.

Significance: The Wanderer’s path is a microcosm of the entire topology; the hidden cross‑reference to the Western Librarian begins to form.

The Council‑Tribunic Wars & The Inquisitor

Abs. 32,588 – 34,085

The Interdimensional United Realms fractures as the Council (Four Realms) and Tribune (Covert Orders) mistake coordination for subordination. The Inquisitor, Redford Heinsforth, exceeds his mandate, performing retroactive arrests, torture, and experiments on Aneian prisoners. Ren discovers the Depths of Abyss laboratory. Heinsforth is tried and sentenced to Tartarus, leading to the Accord of Arcadia and the founding of the Watchers.

Significance: Demonstrates that neutrality without vigilance becomes complicity; the Watchers become the institutionalized Eye.

The Essence of Convergence (Book 6)

Abs. 51,015 – 51,235

The Firmament cracks widen. The Five Personas manifest: Power (Gael becomes a living node), Will (distributed to the Watchers), Becoming (Heinsforth transforms into the Warden), Sacrifice (Gael dissolves completely), and Continuity (Lucius himself). Lucius is banished to the Meadow Realm to distract the Destroyer. The Dark Realm is reorganized as the Guardian Domain, a neutral Fifth Realm.

Significance: The center holds through distributed sacrifice; the Wanderer’s banishment becomes the prelude to the greatest meeting.

Western Plane Chronicles — The Library of Lumeria

Foundation & The Summoning of the Fourth Librarian

Abs. 1 – 20,001

Kronos (Time) and Logos (Wisdom) found the Library of Lumeria. The Primordial Eight and Ethereal Five establish the First Covenant. After the Silence of the gods, the Aurelius Empire rises and falls. In Abs. ~4,987, a young scholar from a world of boundary‑trees — Lucien Jela (Lucien Elder) — is summoned across probability. He becomes the Fourth Librarian, establishes the Triadic Governance, and wields the Metaphysical Embargo (the Restriction).

Significance: The Library becomes the neutral archive of all planes, and its keeper carries a seed of the Old Tree.

The Great Divide & The Isolation of the East

Abs. 20,001 – 28,500

The Xi Dynasty rises in the Eastern Continent of the Western Plane. Lucien imposes the first Metaphysical Embargo (the Great Decay) for perverting cultivation. The Five Kingdoms, the Federation of Sixteen Nations, and the Legion of Argos emerge. In Abs. 28,223, the Great Divide severs all land routes between the Eastern Continent and the Central/Western Continents. The Library’s Eastern Sector is cut off, and the Five Domains formalize isolation.

Significance: The Western Plane forgets the East for millennia, while the seeds in Lumeria pulse with a three‑part harmony.

The Synthesis Wars & The Compromise of the Seven Seals

Abs. 28,500 – 39,660

The Association of Scientific Studies (Seventeen Archipelagos) and the Council of the Arcane clash over the nature of reality. The Rationalist Manifesto tries to eliminate the Intuition node; the Council’s Silence Working disables instruments. Lucien brokers the Interim Concordat and the Synthesis Colleges. The Compromise of the Seven Seals preserves Intuition as “the Preserve of the Ineffable,” ending the Topology Wars.

Significance: Establishes the Dual Path pedagogy that will later become the Academia of Lumeria.

The Library War & The Great Exclusion

Abs. 39,660 – 47,495

During Lucien’s first journey to the Meridian Domain, a jurisdictional schism splits the Library (the Library War). The Concordance of Jurisdictions restores order. Later, Lucien imposes the Second Metaphysical Embargo (the Great Exclusion) on a corrupt rump state. The Neo‑Imperium rises and falls. Meanwhile, the Institute of Psionic Studies transforms into the Tellerium High Society, and the Burning of Xeria (first Unbecoming manifestation) foreshadows the Two‑Millennia War.

Significance: The Library enters 2,282 years of seclusion; the seeds quicken as the Convergence nears.

The Two‑Millennia War & The Trials of Tellerium

Abs. 47,495 – 49,662

Tellerium High Society wages war against the United Realms (Legion, Republic of Argos, Ming Dynasty, Federation). The Unbecoming spreads. After Tellerium’s defeat, the Trials of Tellerium prosecute war criminals (Helena Cross and Kael Varus executed). The Union is proclaimed under Gu Zu, and the Dominion Republic of Argos is founded. Gaius of the Eastern Plane is appointed Fifth Librarian, and Lucien retires to the Meridian Domain.

Significance: Justice and synthesis lay the groundwork for the final Convergence; the Academia of Lumeria is co‑founded.

The Bridge — The Meeting of Librarian and Guardian

The Revelation at the Meadow Realm

Abs. 51,235 (5th Myriad, Year 8,650)

“The Elder returns to the Elder. The Wood remembers the Wood.”

Banished to the Meadow Realm to distract the Destroyer, the Wanderer Lucius Arogzan walks through the endless grass — and finds the one he has dreamed of for five thousand years. Lucien Jela, the Fourth Librarian, retired custodian of the Old Tree, stands waiting. In that space between the Two Trees, the truth is spoken: Lucius was Lucien’s student in a former life, on a distant world of boundary‑trees and quiet shelves. Killed in an accident, he was reborn into the Eastern Plane, carrying only resonances — the Eye, the need to organize, the ache of a library he could not name. Lucien gifts him a crystallized seed containing the memory of fifty thousand years.

The Last Observer appears and declares that the Three Trees (Order, Chaos, and the Old Tree) will soon converge. The Librarian and the Guardian are the bridge: one the catalog, one the story. Their meeting completes the cross‑reference that Eida Jada has tracked for millennia.

Significance: The central mystery of the saga is unveiled — the Wanderer’s strange perspectives, his dreams of shelves and lenses, his innate mastery of the Eye all stem from a teacher who crossed planes to save him. Purity without relation is undone by love that survives death and rebirth.

The Convergence & The Sixth Myriad

The Three Trees Meet

Abs. 51,235+ (6th Myriad)

The Tree of Order (Eastern Plane), the Tree of Chaos (Western Plane), and the Old Tree (Meridian Domain) prepare to converge. The Firmament seals fully. The Guardian Domain becomes a permanent neutral Fifth Realm, governed by the Watchers. In the West, Gaius leads the Academia of Lumeria under the Dual Path. The seeds in the Vault pulse with three‑part harmony. Lucius returns to the Eastern Plane, delivers the Essence of WILL, and teaches a new generation — always adding the quiet lesson of layered truth. Lucien returns to his origin world (Earth) at the exact moment he left, carrying a seed of the Old Tree and writing a new thesis: The Library as Liminal Space.

The Last Observer speaks: “The 14th Eon nears its end. The Convergence of the Two Trees prepares. They have bought us time.” A child is born with violet eyes. The vigil continues.

Final Line of the Saga: “The center holds. For now. For always. As long as we hold it.”

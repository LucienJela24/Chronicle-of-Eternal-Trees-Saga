Other Realms

Celestial domains, liminal spaces, and divine realms

Overview

Beyond the Two Planes exist realms that transcend ordinary geography—the domains of the Five Personas, liminal spaces between realities, and the meeting grounds where the boundaries between existence dissolve.

Divine Domains: Celestial Realm, Great Forest, The Mist, Sanctuary

Liminal Spaces: Tartarus, Meridian Domain, Meadow Realm

The Old Tree: Last remnant of the Wild of Aletheia

Divine Domains (The Five Personas)

Celestial Realm

Apex of the Tree — First Persona's Domain

Domain of Reality, Energy, and Essence. Home of the Protectors: Luminara, Ryūjin, Morvoren, and Mechanus.

Significance: Ground of being; elemental reality. Wanderer's Path sixth clue location.

Great Forest

Branches of the Tree — Second Persona's Domain

Domain of Knowledge, Time, and Space. Home of the Keepers: Goldroad, Orientalis, Occidens, Meridionalis, and Portalis.

Significance: Framework of experience; forest of paths. Nexus of Ways.

The Mist

Roots of the Tree — Third Persona's Domain

Domain of Life and Death. Home of Auriel (Life Bringer) and Umbra (Death Reaper). Where souls journey between lives.

Significance: Meaning through finitude and consequence. Wanderer's Path seventh clue.

Sanctuary

Between the Two Trees — Fourth Persona's Domain

Domain of Mystery and Magic. Prevents the Two Trees from consuming each other. Place of hidden revelations.

Guardian: Fourth Lord (Fourth Persona). Wanderer's Path eighth clue.

Liminal Spaces

Tartarus

Deep Prison within The Mist

Abyss of eternal stillness where the unkillable are sealed. Reformed Dark Emperors and the Inquisitor meditate in silence.

Guardians: Keepers & Death Reapers

Prisoners: Tomoyama, Kross, Marcus, Redford Heinsforth

Meridian Domain

Fourth Lord's Sanctuary

The Old Tree grows here—last remnant of the Wild of Aletheia. Lucien Jela's retirement location.

Inhabitants: Fourth Lord, Lucien Jela, Eida Jada (Copy XII)

Features: Sanctuary of Eight Paths

Meadow Realm

Space Between the Two Trees

The Blood-River flows here. Last Observer's eternal vigil. Where Lucius and Lucien reunite for the Convergence.

Guardian: Last Observer (Aletheia)

Significance: Preparing for Convergence

The Old Tree

The last remnant of the Wild of Aletheia—the primordial forest destroyed in the Civil War. It stands in the Meridian Domain as witness to all that has passed and all that will come.

Nature: The boundary-wood; liminal space between the Two Trees

Significance: Prevents premature convergence; maintains separation

The Elder: Lucien Jela, Fourth Librarian, keeper of the Old Tree

The Wood: Remembers all that the Two Trees have forgotten

Read more about the Old Tree

The Blood-River

Flows through the Meadow Realm, connecting the Two Trees. Formed from the essence of Symphion (Intervenor) and tempered by Aletheia's (Observer) detachment. The river of becoming that carries possibility between Order and Chaos.

Significance: The medium of exchange between the Two Planes; source of all cross-planar travel

The Convergence

At Absolute Year 51,235 and beyond, the Three Trees (Order, Chaos, and Old Tree) prepare to meet. The boundary between Planes begins to dissolve. Lucius and Lucien reunite in the Meadow Realm.

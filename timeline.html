Before Time — The 14th Eon Opening

Before Time

The Endless Seed of Light — self-existent ground of possibility, neither created nor creator, containing infinite potential without prior differentiation.

The First Emergence: The chorus‑consciousness coalesces — a single awareness distributed across multiple forms, undivided, without individual identity.

The First Sorrow: A dream-world shaped by emerging preferences dissolves. Aspects of the chorus experience individual grief for the first time. The first crack in unity appears — birth of individual consciousness within the divine.

The Discovery of Names (1st Eon): Kronathos claims Time — the first named. Spatus (Space), Materia (Matter), Spiritus (Spirit), Aletheia (Truth), Symphion (Harmony), and Cosmarchos (Order) follow. The chorus becomes a pantheon.

The First Covenant (2nd Eon): The First Named establish Sovereignty, Hospitality, and Contest as governing principles. A fragile peace built on compromise.

The Great Question (3rd Eon): "What is creation for?" Cosmarchos argues for control, Symphion for guidance, Aletheia for freedom. The philosophical schism emerges.

The Age of Seeding (4th–7th Eons): Three World Trees are created: Throne of Cosmarchos (crystalline perfection), Garden of Symphion (adaptive growth), Wild of Aletheia (radical contingency).

The Void Wars (8th–11th Eons): The Great Void awakens. The three factions form the Unthinkable Alliance. The Firmament is built through mass divine sacrifice — thousands of First Named pour their essence into the boundary.

The Civil War of the Gods (12th Eon): Alliance fractures. Genocide of the Observers begins. The Wild of Aletheia is systematically destroyed realm by realm.

The Last Working of Aletheia: In the Meadow Realm, Aletheia gathers the essence of every unmade Observer. Her blood becomes the Blood‑River; her consciousness becomes the Last Observer. The Five Personas emerge.

The Sealing of the Fifth Persona (13th Eon): The Last Observer seals Order and Chaos, binding them until creation is ready. The Two Trees — Order and Chaos — are planted in the Meadow Realm. The Vigil begins.

Absolute Year 1 — The Seeding of the Two Planes

Abs. 1 Eastern Western

The Tree of Order violently embeds itself into the Eastern Plane, tearing fissures in reality through which the Void leaks. Simultaneously, the Tree of Chaos takes root in the Western Plane.

EAST: The Ancient Ones — Primus, Solara, Terrakin, Aqualis, Ventus, Aetherius, Terram — emerge. They perform the Rite of the Foundation: each binds to the geography to stabilize reality. Primus leaves his exposed hand as the Stone Table. Solara is imprisoned in the Eternal Flame. The First Generation is born — grey‑tinted skin, golden eyes — ancestors of all human lineages including the Aneians.

WEST: The Primordial Eight tear themselves into existence: Kronos (Time), Logos (Wisdom), Freya (Life/Fate), Kairos (Opportunity), Archelaus (Force/Magic), Nyx (Night/Darkness), Eres (War/Struggle), Artemis (Light/Revelation). Primal war ends when Freya speaks "Enough" — first silence establishes primal truce.

Primordial Era — The Silent Growth (Abs. 1 – 2,584)

Abs. 1 – 800 Eastern The First People Emerge

Abs. 1–100 — Lux‑Children (North): Albino features, photosensitive eyes. Discover the Eternal Flame; Cult of the Flame emerges.

Abs. 1–150 — Stone‑Blooded (East): Grey skin, carry Terrakin's ichor. Develop first crude chi‑cultivation. Five clans: fire, water, earth, wind, and Aether‑touched.

Abs. 1–200 — Echo‑Born (West): Fluid features, memory‑shifting abilities. First "memory‑drowning" case. First Mystics emerge.

Abs. 1–250 — Number‑Touched (South): Analytical minds. Build calculation circles. Discover null properties.

Abs. 1–300 — Threshold Tribe (Center): Maintain the Stone Table on Arcadia Isle. Navigate the Shroud. Become memory‑keepers of the Primordial Era.

Abs. 487 — First Ethereal‑Human Contact: Threshold Tribe Mist‑Walker encounters Kyris in a probability stream.

Abs. 612 — Aris the Sorrowful: Communes with Eternal Flame; learns Solara is imprisoned. Establishes Cult of the Eternal Radiance as a maintenance rite. Becomes First Master of Light.

Abs. 800 – 1,500 Eastern The Schisms

Abs. 800–1,000 — War of the First Elements: Five Primal Shapers clash. Hayate kills Tamashii. Tamashii's essence becomes Aether. First Aether Spring appears.

Abs. 890 — Luminara the Heretic: Attempts to extinguish the Eternal Flame to free Solara. Executed. Followers become the Eclipse, hiding in Catacombs of the Unnamed.

Abs. 1,000–1,200 — Survival Cantons form: Spark Domain (Fire), Mist Valley (Water), Stonehaven (Earth), Sky Peak (Wind), Aether Spring (Aether).

Abs. 1,445 — Glaston the Categorator: Performs Fivefold Binding at Standing Stones. Dies consumed by the Arcane. Creates first Hollow breach. Proto‑Guilds form.

Abs. 1,500 – 2,400 Eastern The Crystallization

Abs. 1,500–2,000 — Lux Imperio formalized: Dual Crown system (Pope + Emperor). Grand Matriarch emerges as third power.

Abs. 1,600–2,100 — Chuugoku unified: Dragon Throne established. First Great Lama emerges. Five Great Houses formalized.

Abs. 1,700–2,200 — Avalon formalized: Seven Kingdoms under High King's Ward Sovereignty. Council of Magisters (49 members). Enchantment and Engineering guilds suppressed.

Abs. 1,800 — First Drome refuses an order: Morko refuses to demolish a bridge. Dromes awaken to full consciousness.

Abs. 2,000–2,400 — Helron State formalized: Triumvirate (Director / President / High Command). Four Research Directorates.

Abs. 2,134 — Helron the Measurer completes Calculation of Decay. Exiled and executed. Followers become Filwind Precursors.

Abs. 2,200 — The Aneian Exile: Most severely mutated First People driven into Dark Realm. Drev Arogzan I establishes Periphery's Edge. Eight Great Families established.

Abs. 2,400 — Destroyer's First Whisper. Prophecy of the 25th received: "When the star born of void and light walks between worlds, the scattered children shall be gathered."

Abs. 2,584 Eastern Covenant of Lies

Representatives agree to: (1) formalize the Four Realms and begin 1st Myriad calendar in Abs. 2,586; (2) propagate the myth of the "Noble Withdrawal"; (3) seal all Primordial Era records in the Meridian Vault beneath the Stone Table; (4) establish Arcadia as hidden covenant ground. The lie is institutionalized.

Abs. 2,585 Eastern

Mera Who Walked the Mist performs the final rite at the Stone Table. She touches the eight cups: "The center holds. Let it be measured now." At midnight, the 1st Myriad begins. Master Oren passes the Yew Staff to Gallian and walks into the Mist.

Eastern Plane — The Five Myriads (Abs. 2,585 – 51,235)

1st Myriad — The Age of Founding (Abs. 2,585 – 12,584)

Abs. 2,586 – 3,712 Gallian's Covenant & The First Hollow Breaches

Abs. 2,586 (1st Myr 0): Gallian receives the Yew Staff. Departs to observe Four Realms. Meets Celestine (North), Tomoyama (East), Kross (West), Marcus (South).

Abs. 2,589 (1st Myr 3): First Covenant forged at Stone Table. Eight guardians — four visible, four Hidden. "To the space between. We do not claim the center. We hold it."

Abs. 2,785–3,285 (1st Myr 200–700): Four Realms harden. Celestine executed (Abs. 2,900); students establish Order of the Star. Tomoyama establishes Root Sanctuary. Kross formalizes Havenfire Guild. Marcus establishes Filwind Institute.

Abs. 3,712 (1st Myr 1,127): First Hollow Breaches. Seventeen fracture points at Meridian Threshold. Lord‑Type Hollow manifests. Twins fall — Black Twin unmade, White Twin dissolves sealing breach. Silent One speaks narrative gap, becomes part of Stone Table. Breaches across all Four Realms contained but not sealed. Seeds of Winter planted.

Abs. 3,713 – 9,588 Institutionalization, Corruption & Gallian's Sacrifice

Abs. 3,735–4,385: Four Covert Orders formally institutionalized with hierarchies and doctrines.

Abs. 4,500 (1st Myr 1,915): 5‑Star Battalion founded by Kaelen First‑Star. Nimorak provided as bridge entity. Recruits undergo Rite of Bonding; eyes turn golden.

Abs. 4,585 (1st Myr 2,000): School of Arcadia founded. Gallian establishes curriculum: Eye Practice, Eight Nodes, hybrid techniques.

Abs. 5,000 (1st Myr 2,415): Ancients warn Destroyer gathers. 5‑Star Battalion sealed in temporal stasis. Will sleep for over 26,000 objective years.

Abs. 7,595–8,385 (1st Myr 5,010–5,800): Corruption of the Guardians. Whispers from Nimorak‑Prime reach Tomoyama, Kross, and Marcus. All three fall. Become Dark Emperors: Demon Emperor, Shadow Emperor, Spawn Emperor.

Abs. 9,585 (1st Myr 7,000): Gallian confronts Dark Emperors at Depths of Abyss. Seals them with Pact‑anchors — not by force, but by reminding them of who they were. Cast into Tartarus.

Abs. 9,588 (1st Myr 7,003): Gallian's Sacrifice. Body dissolves; consciousness diffuses into Meridian Domain. Last words: "The center holds. For now. For always. As long as we hold it."

2nd Myriad — The Myriad of Shadows (Abs. 12,585 – 22,584)

Abs. 19,588 – 19,592 (2nd Myr 7,003–7,007) The Grey Tide & Conclave of Shadows

Abs. 19,588: Grey Tide first encountered — grey mist that consumes meaning. Spreads across all Four Realms. Theron realizes it is the consequence of the Covenant of Lies.

Abs. 19,589: Conclave of Shadows convenes at Arcadia. All delegations arrive: Four Covert Orders, Aneians, Ethereals, Drakes, Mystics, Dromes. Meridian Vault opened — truth revealed: Aneians are the originals. Covenant of Shadows ratified. Celestine places pale gold seed on Stone Table.

Abs. 19,590–19,591: Aileen (Saintess of the North) and Drev Arogzan I unite. Aileen conceives Lucius Arogzan. Seals on Tartarus crack; Dark Emperors stir.

Abs. 19,592 (2nd Myr 7,007): Octagram founded by Selene of Lux Imperio. Lucius Arogzan born — one gold eye (Void‑Touch), one violet eye (prophecy mark). Celestine passes.

Abs. 23,507 (3rd Myr 922): Rite of Balancing catastrophe. Seals on Tartarus shatter; Dark Emperors break free. Lucius caught in surge — mismatched eyes permanently awakened.

The Convergence — Absolute Year 51,235

Abs. 51,235 Both Planes 5th Myr 8,650 · THE CONVERGENCE

The Tree of Order and Tree of Chaos meet in the Meadow Realm. Their roots intertwine across the Blood‑River. The Old Tree — preserved since the Civil War of the Gods — anchors them both. The Five Lords emerge. Primus is purified. The Dark Emperors accept penance. The Last Observer sacrifices itself, releasing the Old Tree. Gallian becomes the new watcher of the Meadow Realm.

Lucien Jela (Fourth Librarian of Lumeria) steps through a portal from the Meridian Domain. He sees Lucius Arogzan — the student he lost on Earth, reborn in the Eastern Plane. Lucius sees Lucien — the teacher he remembers only in dreams.

Lucien speaks first: "Luke. I remember you."

Lucius: "Dr. Elder. I dreamed of you."

The Last Observer (Aletheia) watches from the threshold: "The 14th Eon ends. The Two Trees will meet. The Vigil continues — but in a new key."

Lucien learns his true origin: he was summoned from the Old Tree, from Earth, by Kronos and Logos in the First Myriad. He is free to return home. Lucius completes his purpose and returns to wandering. The bridge between planes is established.

"The center holds. For now. For always. As long as we hold it."

6th Myriad — Post‑Convergence (Abs. 51,236+)

Abs. 51,236+ Both Planes

The Great Divide closes. New thresholds between planes open. Eastern and Western civilizations begin cultural exchange.

Guardian Domain and Library of Lumeria establish formal diplomatic relations. The Academia of Lumeria expands to accept students from both planes.

Sixth Librarian (Elara Vines) appointed (Abs. 51,240). Lucien returns to Earth via the Elder Path — arrives October 14, 2024, at the exact moment he left, carrying a crystallized seed of the Old Tree.

Lucius continues wandering both planes. The Three Trees — Order, Chaos, and the Elder Wood — flourish. The Watchers witness. The Emperors serve. The Library preserves. The Academia teaches. Gallian watches from the Meadow Realm. Lucien tends the boundary-wood on Earth.

6M Y3000: A new generation of pilgrims visits the Meadow Realm. "The center holds because we hold it together. Not because one holds it, but because many have become the holding."

Timeline of the Eternal Trees

A fully synchronized chronicle spanning six Myriads — Absolute Years 1 to 51,235+

Based on the corrected alignment: Eastern Plane includes a 2,585‑year Primordial Era; Convergence occurs simultaneously at Abs. 51,235

Eastern Plane Timeline (Tree of Order)

Abs. 1 – 2,585 Primordial Era / Book 0.5

The Silent Growth: The Tree of Order is planted. Ancient Ones perform the Rite of the Foundation. First People (Lux‑Children, Stone‑Blooded, Echo‑Born, Number‑Touched) form survival cults. Aris the Sorrowful establishes the Cult of the Eternal Flame. The Five Primal Shapers war; Tamashii’s essence becomes Aether. Glaston’s Fivefold Binding cages wild magic. Helron builds the First Hall of Reason. The Aneians are exiled to a pocket dimension. The Covenant of Lies (Abs. 2,584) seals the violent origins and begins the Myriad calendar.

Abs. 2,586 – 7,585 1st Myriad / Book 1

Gallian’s Covenant: Gallian meets Master Oren and gathers the first eight Guardians at Arcadia. The Four Realms harden. The School of Arcadia is founded (Abs. 4,586). First Hollow Breach (Abs. 3,712) – Void‑touched scouts appear. Gallian leads the first defense; the covenant is renewed (Abs. 3,713). The Four Covert Orders are seeded: Order of the Star (Abs. 3,735), Root Sanctuary (Abs. 3,785), Havenfire Guild (Abs. 4,085), Filwind Institute (Abs. 4,385).

Abs. 7,585 – 12,583 Late 1st Myriad / Book 1.5

Grenlian War & Fall of Guardians: Six millennia of warfare against Ordered Void manifestations. The 5‑Star Battalion (100,000 Void‑adapted warriors) is sealed near the Firmament at Abs. 12,583. Three Guardians – Tomoyama, Kross, Marcus – are corrupted into the first Dark Emperors. The Sealing Armada (Abs. 19,588) ends the war; Gallian sacrifices his physical form to maintain the Meridian Threshold.

Abs. 19,588 – 22,584 2nd Myriad / Book 2

Grey Tide & The Conclave: The Grey Tide manifests. The four Covert Orders formally ally with the Allied Races (Ethereals, Drakes, Mystics, Dromes). Celestine is exiled to the Dark Realm on a secret mission. The Octagram is founded at the Meridian Threshold (Abs. 19,592).

Abs. 22,585 – 32,584 3rd Myriad / Book 3

Rite of Balancing & The Wanderer’s Birth: The Rite of Balancing (Abs. 23,507) catastrophically fails. Lucius Arogzan is born, marked by Void‑touch and haunted by inexplicable dreams. The Dark Emperors break free (Abs. 23,508) and wage a two‑century war. The Guardian Order is founded on Arcadia (Abs. 23,712). The Battle of Nexus (Abs. 23,740) defeats the Emperors, who are sealed in Tartarus (Abs. 23,750).

Abs. 23,751 – 23,755 Book 4

Eight Clues: Lucius walks the spiral path: Shadow (Pact), Breath (Self), Wind (World), Depth (Inheritance), Uncertainty (Science), Absence (Intuition), Transcendence, and Center (Artifice). He meets the Fourth Lord in the Sanctuary, receives the Essence of WILL, and becomes the 25th Guardian. He begins training Gael and Ren in the Eye Practice.

Abs. 32,585 – 42,584 4th Myriad / Book 5

Political Fracture & The Inquisitor: The Interdimensional United Realms (IUR) fractures. The Inquisitor Redford Heinsforth exceeds his mandate, conducting arrests, torture, and experiments. Ren discovers the Depths of Abyss laboratory. Heinsforth is tried and sentenced to Tartarus. The Accord of Arcadia (Abs. 40,819) and the Watchers’ Charter are ratified. The 5‑Star Battalion returns (Abs. 41,085) and warns of the Destroyer’s imminent assault.

Abs. 42,585 – 51,235 5th Myriad / Book 6

The Five Personas: Dark Realm‑Inquisitor War (Abs. 51,015–51,085). Essence of POWER: Gael becomes a Firmament node (Abs. 51,025). Essence of WILL distributed to the Watchers (Abs. 51,040). Essence of BECOMING: Heinsforth transforms into the Warden. Essence of SACRIFICE: Gael dissolves completely into the Firmament (Abs. 51,055). Lucius is banished to the Meadow Realm. The Firmament seals fully. The Guardian Domain is established as a neutral Fifth Realm (Abs. 51,085). Western Tree seed grows in the Covenant Forge (Abs. 51,185).

Abs. 51,235 THE CONVERGENCE

Three Trees meet: The Tree of Order, Tree of Chaos, and the Old Tree converge in the Meadow Realm. Lucius writes the final entry of The Wanderer’s Path. The Last Observer announces the 14th Eon’s end. A child with violet eyes is born in the Guardian Domain. The center holds – for now, for always.

Western Plane Timeline (Tree of Chaos / Library of Lumeria)

Abs. 1 – 20,000 Book 7

The Seeding & The Library: Tree of Chaos planted. Primordial Eight and Ethereal Five emerge. First Covenant (Abs. 500). Kronos establishes the Library of Lumeria (Abs. 5,000). Logos becomes Second Librarian and merges with the Index (Abs. 10,000). The Silence begins. Aurelius Empire rises and falls (Abs. 12,000–16,723). Lucien Jela (Lucien Elder) summoned from Earth (Abs. ~4,987), trained for 15,000 years. Zeno serves as Third Librarian. At Abs. 20,001, Lucien is formally appointed Fourth Librarian and establishes Triadic Governance.

Abs. 20,001 – 28,500 Book 8

Eastern Bridge & Severance: Xi Dynasty founded (Abs. 20,001). Great Decay (Abs. 20,203) – first Metaphysical Embargo. Xi Dynasty falls; Federation of Sixteen Nations and Republic of Argos emerge. The Legion founded by Aurelius Augustus Titus (Abs. 21,325). Ming Dynasty unifies the Eastern Continent. The Great Divide (Abs. 28,223) – dimensional rift severs all land routes between Eastern and Western Continents. The Five Domains formalize isolation (Abs. 28,500).

Abs. 28,500 – 39,660 Book 9

Science-Magic Wars: Interim Concordat (Abs. 29,660) creates Synthesis Colleges. Paraformer Genesis: First Resonance Catcher developed. Topology Wars (Abs. 31,200–31,800) – Rationalist Purge vs. Silence Working. Compromise of the Seven Seals (Abs. 32,400) preserves Intuition as “The Preserve of the Ineffable.” Lucien’s tenure formally codified (Abs. 39,660).

Abs. 39,660 – 47,495 Book 10

Jurisdictional Crisis & Great Exclusion: Lucien’s first journey to the Meridian Domain (Abs. 39,667). The Library War (Abs. 39,668–39,675) – schism between Vice‑Chair and Chairman. Concordance of Jurisdictions restores order. Great Exclusion (Abs. 40,203) – second Metaphysical Embargo destroys a corrupt rump state. Neo‑Imperium rises and falls (Abs. 40,212–40,330). Tellerium High Society founded (Abs. 47,345). Burning of Xeria (Abs. 47,430) – first Unbecoming manifestation. United Realms forms (Abs. 47,490).

Abs. 47,495 – 51,235+ Book 11

Two‑Millennia War & The Fifth Librarian: Library Seclusion begins (Abs. 47,495). Two‑Millennia War (Abs. 47,502–49,634) devastates the Western Plane. Trials of Tellerium (Abs. 49,636) – Helena Cross and Kael Varus executed. Union proclaimed under Gu Zu. Gaius of the Eastern Plane appointed Fifth Librarian (Abs. 49,662). Lucien retires to the Meridian Domain (Abs. 49,663). Academia of Lumeria founded (Abs. 49,890). Dominion Republic of Argos proclaimed (Abs. 51,200) with Valeria Scipio and Marcus Varus as first Consuls. The seeds stir as the Convergence approaches.

Abs. 51,235+ 6th Myriad

The Convergence Approaches: The seeds in the Central Archive pulse with three‑part harmony (Order, Chaos, Elder Wood). Gaius maintains the vigil. The Library’s strict neutrality is upheld as the Dominion Republic, Union, Neo‑Magic Institute, and Federation seek access to the stirring seeds. The Ship of Reunion is constructed in the Federation. The Chronicle Compact passes into Gaius’s hands alone as the Sixth Myriad truly begins.

Eastern Plane Factions

Tree of Order — Structured, Hierarchical, Crystalline

Overview

The Eastern Plane is governed by the Tree of Order, rooted in the essence of Cosmarchos (the Earlier God of Order). It consists of Four Great Realms, each aligned with a cardinal direction and elemental force, plus the neutral Guardian Domain at Arcadia Isle.

Protector: Luminara (North), Ryūjin (East), Morvoren (West), Mechanus (South)

Primary Nodes: Transcendence, Pact, Self, Inheritance, Science, Intuition, World, Artifice

Covert Orders: Order of the Star, Root Sanctuary, Havenfire Guild, Filwind Institute

Allied Races: Ethereals, Drakes, Mystics, Dromes

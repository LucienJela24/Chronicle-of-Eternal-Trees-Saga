The Endless Seed & Earlier Gods

Endless Seed of Light

The Self-Existent Ground of Possibility

Pre-cosmic source condition; not a deity or object. Infinite potential without prior differentiation—the source from which all Earlier Gods emerged. Eternal and unchanging, the Seed represents pure possibility without form or limitation.

The Ancient Ones — Eastern Plane

First conscious emanational founders of the Eastern Plane's structured creation. Not Earlier Gods—they emerged from the seeded Eastern Plane itself through the Rite of the Foundation.

Primus

The First Ancient

Principal coordinator of early Eastern Plane formation. Central to the Threshold Tribe's foundational memory. Withdrew into structure rather than rulership, becoming the template for all subsequent guardians who serve rather than rule.

Primordial Era Threshold Tribe

Solara

Ancient of Light

Foundation of Lux Imperio's light traditions. Her essence shaped the northern realm's crystalline architecture and hierarchical sacred order. Her binding created the first structured metaphysics of the Eastern Plane.

Lux Imperio Northern Pillar

Terrakin

Ancient of Earth

Foundation of Chuugoku's elemental traditions and material grounding. His binding to the eastern mountains created the resonance between geography and chi that defines the Empire of Elements.

Chuugoku Eastern Pillar

Aetherius

Ancient of Aether

Associated with relation, balance, and the space between. Important in Chuugoku and later metaphysical balancing traditions. His essence flows through the Meridian Threshold and all liminal spaces.

Meridian Domain Center Point

Aqualis

Ancient of Water

Foundation of the fluid traditions that would later influence both Avalon and Helron. Her binding created the rivers and waterways that connect the Four Realms, including the sacred Blood-River's eastern flow.

Avalon Western Pillar

Ventus

Ancient of Air

Foundation of the wind and message traditions. His essence carries communication between realms and enables the breath of life that sustains all mortal beings. His binding created the atmospheric envelope of the Eastern Plane.

Atmosphere Communication

The Eight Nodes of Topology

The invisible skeleton of the Eternal Tree Saga — axes of cosmic organization, ways of standing in relation to reality. Neither moral nor absolute, but topological positions that shape every realm, faction, and soul.

Topological, Not Moral

The Nodes are not ethical categories but positions of relation. When pursued alone — in purity — each Node inverts into its dark shadow: Science without Intuition becomes Madness, Pact without Transcendence becomes Binding. “Purity creates vacuums; relation fills them.”

The void between definitions, unstructured possibility.

Thematic Heart of the Saga

“Purity creates vacuums; relation fills them.”

This truth derives directly from Node theory. When any single Node is pursued in isolation — purity without synthesis — it inverts into darkness. Health, power, and meaning require relation between nodes: Science with Intuition, Pact with Transcendence, Self with World. The topology is the invisible scripture upon which the Two Trees and the Old Tree stand.

Western Plane Factions

Tree of Chaos — Fluid, Experimental, Adaptive

Overview

The Western Plane is governed by the Tree of Chaos, born from Intervenor (Symphion) and Observer (Aletheia) essence. It is a realm of constant change, experimentation, and adaptation—where order emerges from chaos rather than being imposed upon it.

Divine Pantheon: Primordial Eight (Kronos, Logos, Freya, Kairos, Archelaus, Nyx, Eres, Artemis)

Ethereal Five: Vertus, Minerva, Serena, Dionysus, Morpheus

Key Institutions: Library of Lumeria, Academia, Serene Fleet

Great Divide: Abs. 28,223 — severed Eastern/Western contact for 26,000 years
